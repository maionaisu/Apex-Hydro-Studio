## 2025-02-12 - Accessible Composite Forms & Emoji Screen Reader Polish
**Learning:** In PyQt6, `QFormLayout.addRow("String", widget)` auto-creates labels and sets buddies. However, composite form layouts (like `FormRow` utilizing `QHBoxLayout` and manual `QLabel` instantiation) do NOT auto-link the label to the input widget. Additionally, screen readers often struggle with emojis in button text (e.g., "⚡ Eksekusi Matriks").
**Action:** When building composite form components in PyQt6 (like `FormRow`), always manually link the label to its corresponding input using `QLabel.setBuddy(input_widget)` to ensure proper screen reader announcement and keyboard mnemonic focus. Furthermore, strip emojis from accessible names (using targeted unicode regex ranges) for components like `ModernButton` to keep screen reader text clean, while skipping the stripping for icon-only buttons to avoid wiping their labels entirely.
